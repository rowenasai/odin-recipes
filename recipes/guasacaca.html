Guasacaca Recipe

The Venezuelan guasacaca is a flavor-packed powerhouse. It's creamy, smooth, fresh, and so versatile!

It's perfect as a dip, dressing, or spread, and the best part? You can tweak it to fit your taste.

Like it spicier? Add more garlic. Prefer a bit of tang? Increase the vinegar.
It's endlessly adaptable, and that's what makes it a favorite in my kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Avocados: The creamy base of the sauce. Use ripe avocados for the best flavor and texture.
  • Onion: Adds a bit of sharpness and depth. White onion works best, but you can use red for a milder flavor.
  • Green Sweet Pepper: sweetness and a slight crunch.
  • Garlic: Provides a savory punch. Adjust the amount based on your taste.
  • Cilantro: Gives the guasacaca its signature fresh flavor.
  • Lime/Lemon/Vinegar: Adds a tangy brightness. You can use lime, lemon, or vinegar.
  • Salt and Black Pepper: Essential for seasoning.
  • Worcestershire Sauce (optional): Adds a savory depth and umami flavor. Make sure to use a gluten-free version if needed.
  • Corn Oil (or Any Neutral-Tasting Oil): Helps emulsify the sauce and keeps it smooth.

Steps

The best part of this recipe is that it's quick and easy. Two steps and you're set!

  1. Process or Blend

    2. Put everything into a food processor or a blender and process until you get the consistency you want.

    Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

  2. Enjoy!

    3. That's it! Now you can use it as a dipping sauce, drizzle it over grilled meat or spread it on empanadas!

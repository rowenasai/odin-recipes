Papelon con Limon Recipe

There is no better alternative to spend a hot afternoon than sitting at the beach with a tall glass
of Papelon con Limon and an empanada de cazon!

Description

“Papelon con Limon” is a popular drink in Venezuela and other Latin American countries, although the name varies.
In Colombia is known as Aguapanela. “Papelon con limon” is basically a limeade sweeten with a raw piece of sugar
cane, also known as “piloncillo” or “panela”

Ingredients

  • 5 oz. of papelon (piloncillo, or panela)
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups water
  • Lime or lemon slices for garnishing (optional)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. In a small pan bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Using a cheese grater, shred the papelon.
  3. When the water is boiling, add the grated papelon or piloncillo to the pan.
  4. Turn the heat off, stir with a spoon and let it sit for few minutes until the papelon or piloncillo is
    completely dissolved.
  5. Strain to a serving jar.
  6. Add the lime and lemon juices.
  7. Add 4 more cups of water.
  8. Stir.
  9. Serve over ice and garnish with lime slices.
  10. Enjoy!
